Part 1

HIV treatment for pregnant women
Problem 1: How to treat pregnant women with HIV infection without hurting a baby
Problem 2: Destroying the virus during its latent stage

Theoretical methods

Creating a unique multi-step therapy, using the drugs that do not cause miscarriage or birth defects + creating a new medication for the direct destruction of a host virus. Modern antiretrovirals are either too weak or can hurt the fetus. Thus, a new unique medication is required
Answer the question – why side effects appear & how to develop a new unique drug (computational chemistry – part 2)
Destruction of reservoir cells (using different models)
Future pregnancy. SCNT, GIFT, IVF
