Computational Chemistry Research

Explore the intricacies of organic reactions through my research, where I employ molecular optimizations and high-performance computer calculations. Leveraging the Avogadro program, I meticulously optimize molecule geometries and determine their structures and transition states. Through comprehensive analysis, this study unveils the reaction mechanism, pathway, and energy profile, offering invaluable insights into the reaction's behavior and properties. Excitingly, the results reveal the reaction to be energetically favorable, laying solid groundwork for future studies in the design and optimization of novel molecules and chemical reactions.
